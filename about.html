My Story

Who I Am

Hi there! I'm Ahlumile Totose, a passionate and curious learner who recently discovered the wonderful world of web development. I am currently studying at LifeChoices Academy/YouthCode, where I was introduced to Computer programming for the first time.

What started as a class assignment quickly turned into a genuine interest. There's something incredibly satisfying about writing a few lines of code and seeing a real webpage appear in your browser. It feels like magic, and I'm hooked!

I come from a background in Science, and I believe that combining creative thinking with technical skills opens up incredible opportunities. Web development feels like the perfect bridge between the two worlds.

When I'm not learning to code, you'll find me producing music , reading, listening to music, gaming, or exploring new ideas. I believe that staying curious and well-rounded makes for a better problem-solver and developer.

My Path

My Learning Journey

Every great developer started as a beginner. Here's how my story unfolded.

🌱

Month 1 — March, 2026

The Beginning: Hello, World!

I wrote my very first line of Python. The feeling of seeing "Hello, World!" on the screen was exhilarating. It was the spark that ignited my passion for coding.

🎨

Month 2 — March, 2026

Discovering HTML & CSS

I created my first webpage using HTML. It was basic, but it was mine! Then I added CSS and watched it come to life with colours, fonts, and spacing. It was like painting with code.

🎨

Month 2 — April, 2026

Mastering CSS

I dove deeper into CSS, learning about the box model, positioning, and styling. I experimented with colors, fonts, and layouts to make my pages look more polished and professional.

📐

Month 3 — [May, 2026]

Animated Product Showcase

I built a product showcase page with animated effects using css. It featured Manchester United merchandise, and I had fun experimenting with different animations to make the products pop.

📱

Month 4 — [May/June, 2026]

JavaScript & GitHub

I learned the basics of JavaScript. also learned more about GitHub, version control, and started sharing my projects online. It was exciting to see my code live on the web!

🚀

Now CURRENTLY

Building This Portfolio!

The final project — bringing everything together into a complete personal portfolio website. This represents the culmination of everything I've learned so far, and it's just the beginning!

Looking Back

My Reflection

"Learning to code has been one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences of my life. Every error message taught me something new, and every working page felt like a small victory."

This journey has taught me not just technical skills, but also patience, persistence, and the importance of breaking big problems into small pieces. I've learned to embrace making mistakes as part of the process.

If I could give advice to someone just starting out: just start. Don't wait until you feel "ready." The learning happens by doing, failing, searching, and trying again.
